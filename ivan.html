MY FILM LIST

3 Idiots | 2009

comedy, drama

★★★★★


"Before Everything Everywhere All at Once, this was the life changing movie I held ever since I was a child. It has comedy, it has drama and it has musical numbers. It tackles the flaws of education and the pressure it exerts on students and the expression in the joys of life aka All is well"

A Silent Voice | 2016

teen drama

★★★★★


"A film that I like to claim depicts depression, atonement and acceptance at its finest. Despite the triggering topics, it is an uplifting film that shows the good in everyone, even the heinous ones.”

Everything Everywhere All at Once | 2022

action, adventure, drama, comedy, sci-fi

★★★★★


"Life changing movie. Turn off your brain and you will enjoy the action scenes, production (it is a multiverse) and comedy. Turn on your brain and you will enjoy nihilism, family reconciliation, relationship mending and drama. This movie has everything in it in a sense that it is thrown at you all at once, from crying to laughing, basically coming everywhere throughout the entirety of the movie."

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse | 2018

action, adventure

★★★★★


"The start of the era of recognizing animation in film. It has been a long time coming and finally the world acknowledges film as films and not a subcategory of it. Spider-Verse reinvents the hero films for me since I lost interest in it for a long time and the animation, story, music, characters and more are top notch.”

Hereditary | 2018

drama, horror, thriller, supernatural

★★★★


"A horror film that does not rely on cheap jumpscares and tropey characters. Every character feels real and every decision is in line with what is happening.

I Want To Eat Your Pancreas | 2018

teen drama

★★★★


"Along the sea of films about a person with terminal illness, this one is unique. The focus is not the one with the illness but the other person and how it affected their life both positively and negatively. The visuals, music, story, music and the twist are overall great. Don’t let the name fool you."

Perfect Blue | 1997

drama, horror, psychological thriller

★★★★


"Horror anime usually does not work properly especially horror without any supernatural but I find it scarier that the scare this film tackles feels real that can almost happen to anyone and it is portrayed wonderfully. It also happens that this film is more prevalent in our times and that makes it scarier."

The Book of Life | 2014

adventure, comedy, fantasy

★★★★


"A fun, good time, musical adventure about life and death. Animation still looks great and the musical numbers are still stuck inside my head. Especially that No Matter Where You Are song."

The Cabin in the Woods | 2011

comedy, horror, thriller

★★★★


"Take all the tropes of every horror film and flip that script around. The production value and the overall plot twist is the star of this film."

WALL-E | 2008

adventure, sci-fi

★★★★


"The only Disney film that I truly loved ever since I saw it when I was a child. The animation still holds up and the story translates smoothly."

9 | 2009

adventure, thriller, sci-fi

★★★


“Despite the childish look of the characters, this is a post apocalyptic sci-fi film about the end of humanity and the search of 9 characters trapped inside dolls to know the truth of the destruction. The animation aged poorly but the story is good despite the glaring flaws it has such as forgotten plot points and such.”

The Girl Who Leapt Through Time | 2006

comedy, drama, romance, sci-fi

★★★


“It is weird how a lot of people liked this movie. I did not like it back when I watched it when I was a teen but rewatching it now I still actually do not like it. The consequences for the main character are non-existent and the ending does not make any sense. Either this movie is not good or I am dumb to understand it. ”
